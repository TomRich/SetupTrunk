I regularly get asked what equipment I use to produce my content. Instead of a half-hearted answer, I decided to do this. You'll find an updated list of the equipment I'm currently using, along with links to purchase items.

13" MacBook Air

My MacBook Air comes almost everywhere with me. I do most of my work, that doesn't require a more powerful machine, on here. It's light, portable and extremely snappy. My top choice for a notebook.

Purchase: amzn.to

Mac Mini (2013)

This is my editing machine. I've done a video series specing out a 2013 Mac Mini, and this is the result. My MacBook just can't cope with 1080p video files, so one of this Mac is a must.

Purchase: amzn.to

Logitech Performance MX

I stand firm on the belief that a good amount of my productivity is down to using this mouse. Hugely customisable, tracks on any surface and as ergonomic as you could think of. A must have.

Purchase: amzn.to

A-Technica ATH-M50

My headphones of choice. The ATH-M50s are without doubt the best monitors you can find under $200, if you haven't ever treated yourself to a decent pair of headphones, these are the ones you should get.

Purchase: amzn.to

Red iPhone 5(s)

My iOS device that keeps me coming back. Currently it's an iPhone 5 which has been anodised red by MendMyi, but it could just as easily soon be a 5S. There's a reason Apple are so successful.

Purchase: amzn.to

HTC One

The devil's advocate to my iPhone. The HTC One is currently my daily driver, altough I do switch back to iOS to give myself a break from Android. If you've never tried out an Android handset before, the HTC One is a great place to start.

Purchase: amzn.to

Nikon D3200

Ah, my trusty DSLR. This camera helps me to produce both the video and photo content that I need to on a daily basis. Affordable enough for most people to pick up, good enough quality that it looks professional if you know how to use it.

Purchase: amzn.to

Sigma 30mm f/1.4

This 30mm is my recent upgrade from a 50mm f/1.8, I have no regrets with the purchase. Solid as a tank, and produces images as sharp as many more expensive lenses. Combine that with the quality of it's bokeh, and you've got yourself a winner.

Purchase: amzn.to

My Coffee Machine

Arguably my most critical piece of equipment, this coffee machine makes a killer cup. If you're a coffee drinker, and are still relying on instant; stop that. This is what you need, good value too.

Purchase: amzn.to
